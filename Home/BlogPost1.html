Enter The Dojo

The blog project has rekindled my love for design & programming.While the process was tough I can recall the moments when I cried while performing my day job knowing it was sucking the life out of me.But those moments were needed to find my way back on the path to programming. I decided to put my faith into fruition once again after a long detour from programming and enroll into Udacity's Front-End Developer course! To quote one of today's best musical artist Drake this class went "Zero to 100 real quick!" from the time I sat down I was knee deep in HTML/CSS and on to building a blog for my first major test. I was getting a buffet of knowledge and expierence to help aid me in my journey!
